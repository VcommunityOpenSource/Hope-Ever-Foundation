Core Employability & Add-on Programs

Holistic employability initiatives focused on soft skills, career counselling, Tally, design, driving, and workplace readiness to prepare youth for long-term professional success.

📍 Tamil Nadu 🤝 Hope Ever Foundation & BIRDS 🚗 Career-Oriented Skill Programs

Project Overview

Recognizing that technical skills alone are not sufficient for long-term professional success, Hope Ever Foundation Trust and BIRDS introduced a series of employability-focused add-on programs designed to prepare youth for modern workplace environments.

These initiatives included soft-skills training, career counselling, employee engagement programs, and workplace readiness modules aimed at building confidence, communication, adaptability, and professionalism among trainees.

Specialized Tally and design training modules prepared students for opportunities in accounting, office administration, business support, and creative industries.

In addition, four-wheeler driving courses connected youth to high-demand employment opportunities in logistics, transportation, delivery services, and mobility sectors.

Career counselling sessions and exposure visits created direct interaction opportunities with industry professionals, employers, and workplace environments.

These interactions helped trainees reduce workplace anxiety, understand career pathways, and develop realistic expectations regarding employment opportunities and work culture.

Collectively, these programs bridged the gap between technical training and real-world employability, ensuring that youth were not only employable, but also adaptable, confident, resilient, and prepared for diverse professional settings.

Key Objectives

Strengthen communication and workplace readiness skills.
Prepare youth for accounting, logistics, and creative careers.
Provide career counselling and industry exposure opportunities.
Reduce workplace anxiety and improve professional confidence.
Bridge the gap between technical training and employability.
