Project Overview

Hope Ever Foundation partnered with the Agricultural Sector Skill Council of India (ASCI) to implement a 36-month Organic Farming Livelihood Program aimed at promoting sustainable agriculture among rural farming communities.

The initiative focused on strengthening farmers’ knowledge and practical skills through structured training programs, demonstration plots, and ongoing technical support that enabled farmers to transition from conventional farming methods to sustainable organic practices.

Training modules covered key aspects of organic agriculture including composting, natural pest management, crop rotation, soil health improvement, and organic certification processes.

Field demonstrations played a major role in showcasing the effectiveness of organic farming techniques, encouraging wider adoption among farmers and building confidence in sustainable methods.

The program directly benefited more than 400 farmers, many of whom reported up to a 50% increase in crop yields after adopting organic farming practices.

Improved market linkages further enhanced farmer incomes, as the project facilitated access to premium organic produce markets and connected farmers with buyers interested in sustainable products.

By reducing dependency on chemical fertilizers and pesticides, the initiative supported long-term soil health, environmental sustainability, and healthier farming ecosystems.

Beyond increased income, farmers developed greater resilience, diversified livelihood opportunities, and improved community well-being through sustainable agricultural practices.

Key Objectives

Promote sustainable and environmentally friendly farming practices.
Train farmers in organic cultivation and certification processes.
Improve soil health and reduce chemical dependency.
Strengthen market access for organic produce.
Enhance farmer incomes and livelihood resilience.
