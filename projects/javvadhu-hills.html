Project Detail

Land Holding Pattern & Horticulture Prospects, Javvadhu Hills

Duration: 11 months | Client: State Planning Commission, Tamil Nadu

A comprehensive socio-economic and livelihood assessment among the Malayali Scheduled Tribes in the Javvadhu Hills. The study analyzed landholding, farming systems, and horticulture opportunities with a focus on income enhancement through crop diversification and value addition.
