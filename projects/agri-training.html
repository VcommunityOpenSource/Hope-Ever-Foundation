Agriculture & Livelihoods

Agricultural Training Center & Livestock Development Program

Strengthening the knowledge and capacities of small and marginal farmers through sustainable agriculture, livestock development, water conservation, and participatory community engagement.

⏳ Duration: 24 Months 📍 Tamil Nadu 🤝 Manos Unidas, Spain

Project Overview

The Agricultural Training Center and Livestock Development Program was established to strengthen the knowledge and capacities of small and marginal farmers, especially in the areas of crop management, water conservation, and animal husbandry.

A detailed baseline study was conducted to understand the socio-economic conditions of farmers, agricultural practices, livestock ownership, and the major challenges affecting farm productivity.

The program encouraged participatory resource management through the formation of water committees, especially in drought-prone rural areas. These committees played a key role in improving local water governance and conservation efforts.

Farmers were introduced to improved farming methods, soil conservation techniques, and integrated livestock practices aimed at supplementing household income and improving long-term agricultural sustainability.

Regular training programs were conducted on fodder cultivation, livestock disease management, breeding practices, and modern agricultural methods. These initiatives significantly enhanced livestock productivity and farmer awareness.

Participatory evaluations allowed farmers and stakeholders to continuously review project progress, identify implementation challenges, and adopt corrective strategies collaboratively.

Over a 24-month period, the project contributed to improved agricultural productivity, enhanced food security, and greater resilience among rural farming communities.

Key Objectives

Strengthen crop management and sustainable farming practices.
Improve livestock productivity through training and support.
Promote water conservation and participatory resource management.
Enhance food security and farmer resilience in rural communities.
Build long-term agricultural sustainability through community participation.
