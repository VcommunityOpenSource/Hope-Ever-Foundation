About Hope Ever Foundation

Rooted in Communities, Driven by Evidence

Established in 2012 as a registered trust under the Indian Trust Act, 1882, Hope Ever Foundation works across Tamil Nadu with SC, ST, women, and youth communities for sustainable, inclusive development.

Our History

Our History

Hope Ever Foundation was established in 2012 as a registered non-profit under the Indian Trust Act, 1882. Over the past decade, it has grown into a leading development organization in Tamil Nadu, focusing on the empowerment of marginalized populations including Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), women, and youth. The Foundation's interventions encompass sustainable livelihoods, education, child rights, environmental conservation, and community capacity building. It combines grassroots-level engagement with policy-level advocacy, ensuring interventions are technically sound, socially inclusive, and sustainable.

Recognition & Legal
