Who We Are

Not charity.
Partnership.

Udhav Foundation was built on a simple belief: lasting change only comes when communities are active participants — not passive recipients — in their own development.

From rural Tamil Nadu to the slums of Mumbai, our 1,200 field workers live and work alongside the people we serve. Every program is designed with, not for, the community.
